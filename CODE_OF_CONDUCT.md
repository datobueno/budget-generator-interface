# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We want this project to be a respectful, inclusive, and constructive space for everyone.

## Expected Behavior
- Be respectful and direct.
- Focus on the work, not the person.
- Welcome feedback and review with good faith.
- Assume contributors are trying to help.

## Unacceptable Behavior
- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks
- Discrimination of any kind
- Bad-faith review or deliberate disruption

## Enforcement
Project maintainers may edit, hide, or remove content that violates this code of conduct and may restrict participation when needed.

For conduct issues, contact the maintainers through the repository discussion or issue tracker.

For security issues, do not use public issues. Follow [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md).
